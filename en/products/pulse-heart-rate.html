Pulse·Heart Rate

Your pocket heart rate companion: camera-based measurement, integrated Health data, trends visualization, and alerts.

Key Facts

  • Platform: iOS (iPhone)
  • System: iOS 14+
  • Pricing: Free with IAP
  • Privacy: Developer may collect Health & Fitness data, not linked to identity
  • App Store

Core Features

  • Measure heart rate with the phone camera
  • Auto sync with Apple Health heart rate data
  • View daily/weekly/monthly trends and charts
  • Abnormal heart rate alerts and suggestions

Tips & Disclaimer

  • Keep your finger steady on the camera and ensure adequate lighting
  • This app does not replace professional medical equipment; consult a doctor if needed
